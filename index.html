Ranipuram Hills

Ranipuram Hills is a popular scenic hotpot in Kasaragod district which is situated about 43 km away from Kanhangad.

Possadi Gumpe

Possadi Gumpe is another hillock destination in Kasaragod that gives a picture of the hilly terrains and the rolling hills that chart the district.

Valiyaparamba Backwaters

Valiyaparamba backwaters is a picturesque destination in Kasaragod and is situated 10 km south of Nileswaram.

Ananthapura Lake Temple

As the name suggests, Ananthapura Lake Temple is located in the middle of a lake in the village of Ananthapura.

Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort is a historical structure built in the 17th century and has itself become part of an identity of the district.

Chandragiri Fort

Chandragiri Fort is another historical landmark in the district situated in the south-east of Kasaragod town.

Malik Ibn Dinar Mosque

Malik Ibn Dinar Mosque, located at Thalankara, was built by Malik Ibn Dinar who was one among the first to bring Islam to India.

Nileswaram

Nileswaram holds adequate significance in the map of Kasaragod as it is rightly known as the cultural capital of the district.
